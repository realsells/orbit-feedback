How Scoring Works

Each respondent rates you 1–5 on eight dimensions. Your Orbit Score is the average across all eight, calculated per response and across all responses. Here's what the numbers mean — inspired by 360° feedback frameworks and brand perception models, but made for real humans.

🧊
1.0 – 1.8 · The Cold Orbit
Ghost Mode
People aren't connecting with you — and they might be actively avoiding the conversation. This isn't a verdict, it's a wake-up call. Something about how you're showing up is pushing people to the edges. The good news? You asked, and that already takes guts.
🌤️
1.9 – 2.6 · The Distant Orbit
Fading Signal
You're in the room, but you're not quite landing. People sense something's a little off — maybe some walls are up, or the energy isn't matching the intention. There's real potential here, but the signal needs to get stronger and more consistent.
⚡
2.7 – 3.4 · The Middle Orbit
Solid but Stuck
You're doing fine — and that's exactly the problem. People see you as decent, reliable, nice enough. But you're not quite breaking through to memorable. This is the zone where most people live, and a few intentional shifts can launch you into something special.
🔥
3.5 – 4.2 · The Inner Orbit
Magnetic Energy
Now we're talking. People genuinely like being around you. You're bringing something real — authenticity, warmth, presence — and others feel it. You've got a few edges to smooth out, but make no mistake: you're somebody people want in their corner.
🌟
4.3 – 5.0 · The Core Orbit
Gravitational Force
You are the sun in this solar system. People don't just like you — they're drawn to you. You make rooms better, conversations deeper, and people feel more alive after being around you. This is rare. Protect it, keep growing, and don't let it go to your head.
